February, 17th. Our oldtimers - Rehabitation and Luk!

While the winter still has a lot of teeth to show us, our old friend Rehabitation offers a warm up with the spring's vibe, journey in the mood of forthcoming (and neverending) green season. “Meanwhile in May” is a smooth and soulful half-hour off the routine days which brings us a chance to take a deep breath, lean back and uncover ‘hopeless romantic’ part of soul for a while..

"Rückgriff" is a word from another old buddy - Luk, seasoned vinyl spinner who always prefer to stay closer to the strict minimalistische musik with deep and melancholic flavour. Works well for walk and trip. No autosync, only hands and ears control!
